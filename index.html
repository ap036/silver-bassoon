ABOUT US

Jayapal Rigs is a family owned drilling company was estabilshed in Tiruchengode, Tamil Nadu, India by R.Kulandaivel & K.Kathirvel. Currently Mr.Kathirvel is the Managing director(MD)of the company who started his career as a basic worker in drilling industry & later transformed to a successful entrepreneur by his effortless hard work. The company originally derived from Sri Jayapal Transports and Sri Jayapal Rig Service. Jayapal Rigs have been in drilling industry since working in various private & public sector projects all over India.
